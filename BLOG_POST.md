## How It Works: Protection in Three Simple Steps

### Step 1: Smart Risk Monitoring 🤖
The moment you subscribe, RideShield's AI begins monitoring your city for potential disruptions:
- Rainfall levels
- Temperature extremes
- Air quality index (AQI)
- Local restrictions and curfews

Our system tracks real-time data from multiple sources, ensuring no disruption goes unnoticed.

### Step 2: Automatic Claim Activation 📱
When conditions cross predefined thresholds, the magic happens:
- **Heavy Rain**: Rainfall > 50mm
- **Extreme Heat**: Temperature > 42°C
- **Severe Pollution**: AQI > 400
- **Local Restrictions**: Government-imposed limitations

No need to file a claim. No need to prove you couldn't work. The system knows.

### Step 3: Instant Payout 💰
Within hours, compensation lands in your account:
- Estimated lost income calculated based on your working hours
- Payout capped at your plan limit
- Direct transfer to your digital wallet or bank account

## The Technology Behind the Shield

### AI & Machine Learning
Our risk scoring model analyzes:
- Historical weather patterns across 50+ Indian cities
- Seasonal disruption trends
- City-specific risk factors
- Individual rider working patterns

This data-driven approach ensures fair pricing and accurate payout calculations.

### Fraud Detection
We've built intelligent safeguards:
- GPS location validation
- Delivery activity verification
- Duplicate claim detection
- Pattern recognition for suspicious behavior

### Parametric Triggers
Unlike traditional insurance that requires proof of loss, parametric insurance pays out based on predefined triggers. When rainfall exceeds 50mm, the payout happens—no questions asked, no documentation needed.
